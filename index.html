WELCOME TO TROPICAL FOREST

La Pastora, San Marcos de Tarrazú, Costa Rica
9.628063 ° N, –83.994797 ° W

At Tropical Forest, we specialize in offering authentic experiences in nature, combining adventure, sustainability, and a deep connection with the environment.

Located in the beautiful region of La Pastora, Tarrazú, in the Los Santos Zone, San Marcos de Tarrazú, Costa Rica, we offer activities such as hiking, tree climbing, and picnics on treetops. Each adventure is designed to immerse our visitors in stunning landscapes and provide them with a unique connection to the local biodiversity.

At Tropical Forest, the experience goes beyond just adventure; we also promote active conservation. Through our activities, we aim to raise awareness about the importance of caring for natural resources and fostering responsible tourism.

Our commitment to the environment is fundamental. Every activity is an opportunity to enjoy and learn while contributing to the preservation of the ecosystems in this unique region.

We invite you to experience a genuine ecotourism adventure, full of excitement, learning, and respect for nature! Join us for a unique ecotourism experience!

Ficus Tree Climbing

Climbing a fig tree is not just a physical challenge; it is an inner journey toward freedom, where each step invites you to discover what you are capable of achieving.

Walking along trails that cross mountains, pastures, and tall fruit orchards, the path reveals breathtaking views that... Prepare for what's coming. In front of us rises the Higuerón, a natural wonder that defies gravity and imagination. With your safety gear on, your guide will instruct you to begin the climb. With your hands and feet, you will gain height, challenging your limits and feeling the adrenaline rush in every movement.

Treetop Picnic

The Oak Network

La Net del Roble is an exclusive treetop experience designed for those who seek to immerse themselves in nature’s beauty while embracing the thrill of adventure. Perched high among the branches, it offers a unique perspective of the lush landscapes and rich biodiversity of Costa Rica.
Dare to rise, explore, and connect with nature from above.

After a short hike, you'll come across a majestic oak tree. With the necessary safety equipment, you'll climb 20 meters to the top, where a hand-woven net awaits you, perfect for relaxing,
sharing moments, and enjoying breathtaking views.

At the top, we invite you to experience an authentic Tarrazú experience: high-altitude coffee brewed with exceptionally high-quality regional beans by Café Haug. Enjoy breakfast or snacks with your friends and loved ones.

Las Caras and El Tirrá

OUR PARTNER

Ecos del Bosque is a rainforest reserve and lodge dedicated to preserving biodiversity, promoting sustainability, and strengthening connections between visitors and nature.

Nestled on the lush Osa Canton in Costa Rica's South Pacific, our reserve offers unique opportunities to explore pristine rainforests, engage in sustainable farming practices, and enjoy eco-friendly accommodations designed for comfort and environmental harmony. Join us at Ecos del Bosque for an unforgettable stay where adventure meets sustainability, and every moment contributes to the preservation of our planet's invaluable natural heritage.
